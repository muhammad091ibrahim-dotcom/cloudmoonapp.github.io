eFootball™ is a free-to-play football simulation game developed and published by Konami. It represents the evolution of the Pro Evolution Soccer (PES) series, offering a dynamic and realistic football experience on mobile platforms.

eFootball™ features two primary game modes:
· Authentic Team: Allows players to control real-life football clubs and national teams in offline matches or local multiplayer.
· Dream Team: Enables players to create their own team by signing real players and coaches, customizing their team's formation, kit, and playing style.

The game emphasizes tactical depth, with features like Link-up Play, where two players can combine in special ways to boost their abilities during matches. Additionally, the Kick Cancel command has been introduced to prevent unintended passes or shots.

eFootball™ has a vibrant esports scene, with the eFootball™ Championship Open being the largest official worldwide esports tournament for the game. This tournament is accessible to all players, allowing them to participate in competitive events.

The game is free to play, with in-app purchases available for cosmetic items, player cards, and other enhancements. Players can earn in-game currency through gameplay or purchase it directly.

  • Why does HD sometimes look like SD?

    HD raises stream bitrate—so you see sharper motion. When a frame is static, SD and HD look similar. If the whole stream seems blurry, check your connection stability: cloud gaming needs a more stable link than YouTube.

  • I have fast internet; why do I still lag or stutter?

    Speed alone isn’t enough—low latency & steady ping matter more. Torrents, video downloads or crowded Wi-Fi can spike jitter. Switch to 5 GHz or wired, and pause any large downloads.
