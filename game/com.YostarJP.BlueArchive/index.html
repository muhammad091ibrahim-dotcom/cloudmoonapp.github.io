ブルーアーカイブ (Blue Archive) is the Japanese server version of Blue Archive, the academy × youth × story RPG published by Yostar. The game first launched in Japan on February 4, 2021. In this title, players take on the role of a “Sensei,” guiding unique and charming students through their daily lives in the academy city of Kivotos while resolving various incidents that arise.

ブルーアーカイブ (Blue Archive) is characterized by its real-time 3D battles and beautifully crafted 2D animations. As the advisor of the organization “Schale,” players work alongside their students to tackle the problems faced by different academies.

· Real-Time 3D Battles: Cute characters fill the screen and deliver dynamic, fast-paced combat.
· 2D Animation: Gorgeous animations highlight each character’s charm and bring the story to life.
· Story Progression: The main story is divided into volumes, each delving deeper into the episodes of different academies.

Distinctive Features
· Unique Characters: A wide range of students appear, each with their own backstories and personalities that enrich the narrative.
· Character Growth: Strengthen and develop your students to form powerful teams capable of taking on high-difficulty missions.
· Event Content: Regularly held events provide limited-time stories and rewards to keep the gameplay fresh and engaging.

Cross-Media Expansion
· ブルーアーカイブ (Blue Archive) has expanded beyond the game itself into other media:
· Anime: From April to June 2024, the anime ブルーアーカイブ The Animation aired, further broadening the world of the game.
· Manga: Serialized manga featuring the game’s characters has also been published, gaining popularity among fans.

ブルーアーカイブ (Blue Archive) is a captivating RPG that portrays youth and adventure within the academy city of Kivotos. Together with its distinctive cast of students, embark on a journey to discover the small miracles hidden in everyday life.
